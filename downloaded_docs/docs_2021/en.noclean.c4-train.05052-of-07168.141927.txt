This medication can be used to treat vaginal yeast-based infections. Clotrimazole reduces vaginal burning, itching, and discharge that might occur with this problem. This medication can be an azole antifungal. It works by stopping the growth of yeast (fungus) that causes the infection.
Clotrimazole is employed to treat yeast-based infections of the vagina, mouth, and skin such as athlete’s foot, jock itch, and body ringworm.
Lotrimin AF Cream is an antifungal medication that fights infections caused by fungus. Lotrimin AF Cream (for your skin) is employed to take care of skin infections such as athlete’s foot, jock itch, ringworm, and yeast-based infections.
