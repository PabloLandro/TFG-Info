Studies Combine Imitrex, Aleve In One Tablet For Migraine Treatment
This article was originally published in The Tan Sheet
09 Apr 2007
News
Rebekah Moan r.moan@elsevier.com
Executive Summary
Tablets with a combination of sumatriptan and naproxen sodium relieve symptoms of migraine headaches more effectively than either of the substances used alone or both taken in separate tablets, according to results of two studies conducted with patients across the United States
