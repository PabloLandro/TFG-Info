Clotrimazole is a drug used to deal with serious fungal infections inside the body. Clotrimazole belongs to the group of drugs referred to as azole antifungals.It is an anti-fungal medication used to treat yeast infections of the vagina, skin, and mouth. It is commonly used for athlete’s foot, jock itch, body ringworm, and oral thrush. Clotrimazole works by preventing the growth of fungi by interfering with the production of the membrane that surrounds fungal cells.Lotrimin is one of the brand names of clotrimazole. There are several other brand names, and this medicine is also used in combination products.Clotrimazole comes as a cream, powder, and lotion to apply to the skin.
