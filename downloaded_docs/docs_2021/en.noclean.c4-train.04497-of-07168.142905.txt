A migraine and Its Symptoms
The person suffering from migraine problems must be feeling severe pulsating pain and sensations in the head. In addition, the person may also feel the problems like nausea, vomiting, and sensitivity. It comes in the form of attacks which occurs at irregular time intervals. It includes the warning symptoms which is also known as aura. The following can be the symptoms of migraine disorder.
Flashes of light.
Blind spots.
Tingling on one side of the body part.
Pins or needle sensations in arms or legs.
Difficulty speaking and problems with light and sound.
During the attack, you will feel a one-sided headache or both-sided.
Extra sensitivity to light and sound, and even touch.
Faintness or light-headedness.
Vision loss.
Throbbing pain sensations.
There are other major symptoms with which you can diagnose the migraine disorder You must judge this problem in time and can avoid its major chronic effects beforehand. There can be several reasons or causes behind this problem. These causes may include
Genetic history.
Improper eating habits.
Hormonal changes issues.
Harmful food additives and drinks.
Stress and physical variables.
Improper wake-sleep cycles.
Bad impact of high dosage medicines.
