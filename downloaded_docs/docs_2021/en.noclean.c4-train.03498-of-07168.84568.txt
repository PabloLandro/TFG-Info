Though not
life threatening, warts can be pretty annoying. As much as they go away on
their own, you might decide to use one of the many available natural home remedies for warts removal
methods if the wart becomes painful, embarrassing or starts to spread.
There are
different methods of treating the wart depending on where it is located, type
or the symptoms it exhibits. The goal is to do away with the wart without
leaving behind any scar tissue. Over the counter wart removal products, salicylic
acid, duct tape and freezing are some of the available options.
Over –the
–counter Products
These are
the easiest to use. It is however not natural. So if you’re looking for natural home remedies for warts removal, consider the other methods discussed below. For the over-the-counter products, however, you just buy from your pharmacist and follow the
instructions on the pack.
Many people
swear by the old duct tape method. For it to be effective, ensure that the tape
remains on at all times. You can only remove it for a few hours every week. Any
kind of nonporous tape like the electricity tape will work just fine.
Salicylic
Acid
This method
works by dissolving the protein in the wart and the surrounding skin. They come
in different types from plasters, drops, jells to pads.
Freezing
methods
This method
involves exposing the wart to temperatures below minus 57C. An aerosol spray
can do this. This methods best mimics the liquid nitrogen used by
dermatologists.
