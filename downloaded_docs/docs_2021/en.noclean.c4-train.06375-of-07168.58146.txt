Adult Pain Management
Acetaminophen, the active ingredient in TYLENOL®, is identified as a first-line pain relief option by the:
American Heart Association8 for patients with, or at high risk for, cardiovascular disease
National Kidney Foundation9 for episodic use in patients with underlying renal disease
American Geriatrics Society10 for persistent pain, particularly musculoskeletal pain
Osteoarthritis
Proven analgesic efficacy for patients with OA.11,12
