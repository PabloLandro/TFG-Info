Product photos are available at https://bit.ly/2Y2KTJo OTTAWA, March 18, 2019 /CNW/ – Pur-Natural Ingredients of Canada is recalling Pur Naturals brand products from the marketplace because they contain milk which is not declared on the label. People with an allergy to milk should not consume the recalled products described below. The following products have been sold in Ontario and Quebec.
