Gastational Diabetes Diet Management Protocol: Calculate Ideal Body Weight Start with 100 pounds + 5 pounds per inch over 5 feet Add 30 pounds for pregnancy Convert to Kilograms: Pounds / 2.2 Protocol: Calculate Total Daily Calories Option 1 Calories/day = Ideal Weight (in kg) x 35 KCal/kg Option 2 (if BMI > 30 kg/m2) Calories/day = actual weight (in kg) x 25 Kcal/kg Protocol: Calculate
