Functions: Subdues rebellious Qi. Indications: Cough, asthma, sore throat, pain in the supraclavicular fossa. References: The English Name and Function have been reprinted from  Maciocia: The Foundations of Chinese Medicine , Copyright 1989, with permission from Elsevier.
