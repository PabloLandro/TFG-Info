        surgeries, please see details in center panel. Bariatric Surgeons in Connecticut Gastrointestinal surgery to treat severe clinical obesity is becoming 
        a popular option in the campaign to help patients lower their weight and 
        improve co-morbidities associated with excess body mass index. However, 
        whether you choose stomach bypass, lap-band or vertical banded gastroplasty, 
        be aware that none of these bariatric operations is guaranteed to work 
        unless you adhere to post-operative dietary and fitness guidelines. Due 
        to the severity of the dietary 
        regime after bariatric surgery , be sure to ask your bariatric surgeon 
        about post-surgery support groups and other patient suppport facilities. Finally, before contacting a suitable obesity clinic, consult your doctor 
        about all your options, including non-surgical weight loss methods. Obesity Clinics in
