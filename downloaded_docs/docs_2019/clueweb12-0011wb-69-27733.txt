				Dingchuan Dingchuan Chinese Name : 
Dingchuan (English translation: Stop Asthma) Location: On the back, 0.5 cun lateral to the lower 
border of the spinous process of the 7th cervical vertebra. Indications: Asthma, cough, oppression of the chest, 
	shortness of breath Sore throat Functions: Calms asthma, dispels Wind, stops cough. Needling: Perpendicular insertion .5 - .8
