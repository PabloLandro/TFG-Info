Primary Indications: Respiratory disorders (common cold / bronchitis / asthma / pleurisy / pneumonia / whopping cough), cough, stiff neck, back pain. Tertiary Indications: Headache, nasal obstruction, shoulder pain, fever, jaundice, urticaria, abscess on the back, vomiting, heat sensations. Other Indications: Heavy eye, sneezing, tonsillitis, agitation, infantile malnutrition, hemiplegia, quadriplegia. References: This information is from the book Acupuncture Point Dynamics and has been used with permission from the author John McDonald.
