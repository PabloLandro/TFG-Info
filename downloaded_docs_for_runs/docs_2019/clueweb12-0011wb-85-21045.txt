		        injection (ICSI) Design: Randomized, prospective, controlled clinical study. Setting: University IVF center. Patient(s) : Two hundred twenty-five infertile patients undergoing IVF/ICSI. Intervention(s): In group I, 116 patients received luteal-phase acupuncture according to the principles of traditional Chinese medicine. In group II, 109 patients received placebo acupuncture. Main Outcome Measure(s): Clinical and ongoing pregnancy rates. Result(s): In group I, the clinical pregnancy rate and ongoing pregnancy rate (33.6% and 28.4%, respectively)
