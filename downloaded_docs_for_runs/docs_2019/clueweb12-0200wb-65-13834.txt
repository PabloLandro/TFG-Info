Healing honey evidence revealed 04-07-06:  Scientists have found substantial evidence that demonstrates the healing powers of honey. Honey has long been thought of as a wound remedy but conclusive evidence has been elusive until now. Twenty-two trials were conducted involving 2,062 patients treated with honey. Sixteen additional tests were performed on animals. Honey proved to be beneficial for healing wounds with additional anti-inflammatory properties that also reduced scarring. Read more...
