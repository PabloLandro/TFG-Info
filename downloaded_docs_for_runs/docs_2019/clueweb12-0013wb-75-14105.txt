Nature: Meeting point of Governing Vessel and Stomach. Functions: Calms the mind. Indications: Epilepsy, anxiety, palpitation, insomnia, headache, vertigo, rhinorrhea. References: The English Name, Nature and Function have been reprinted from  Maciocia: The Foundations of Chinese Medicine , Copyright 1989, with permission from Elsevier.
