Functions: Disperses fullness from the chest, stimulates the descending of Lung-Qi, stops cough. Indications: Cough, asthma, pain in the chest, shoulder and arm, fullness in the chest. References: The English Name and Function have been reprinted from  Maciocia: The Foundations of Chinese Medicine , Copyright 1989, with permission from Elsevier.
