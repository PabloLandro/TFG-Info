Canesten Plus Clotrimazole & Hydrocortisone Cream 30g
A combined anti-fungal and anti-inflammatory treatment for athlete's foot to attack the cause of the infection and soothe the associated itch, inflammation, pain and redness. Use with a follow-on treatment of Canesten Clotrimazole Cream to complete the treatment and prevent recurrence.
More Info
Canesten Clotrimazole Anti-fungal Cream 20g & 50g
A broad spectrum anti-fungal cream for the treatment of athlete’s foot. Apply 2-3 times a day until symptoms disappear. Continue use for a further 2 weeks to avoid recurrence. Also available in 50g.
More Info
Canesten Once Daily Bifonazole Athlete’s Foot Cream 20g
A convenient once-daily cream for the treatment of athlete’s foot. Treatment should be continued for a further 2 weeks after symptoms clear to avoid recurrence.
More Info
Canesten Clotrimazole Anti-fungal Topical Solution 20ml
Suitable for hairy areas or as a substitute for creams. Apply 2-3 times a day to the affected area until symptoms disappear. Continue use for a further 2 weeks to avoid recurrence.
