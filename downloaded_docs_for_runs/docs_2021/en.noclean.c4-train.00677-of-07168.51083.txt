ACHILLO-HIT
Zoom
Zoom
Achilles tendon bandage with large surface compression and proprioceptive stimuli
FEATURES
Large surface nubbly silicone pad
Secures with velcro
Expanded special knitted fabric at the heel allows for a virtually wrinkle-free fit when moving
Latex-free
BENEFITS
Restoration of the soft tissue function
Compression and massage of the achilles tendon
Avoids shortening of the calf muscle while deliberatly not elevating the heel
Proprioceptive stimuli on the tibialis posterior tendon
Functional positioning of heelfoot
Reduction of non-physiological strain on the achilles tendon
Correcture of the muscular disbalance
INDICATIONS CONSERVATIVE THERAPY OF "ACHILLODYNIA":
Paratendonitis
Tendonitis
Subachilles bursitis
Achillobursitis
INDICATIONS POST-OPERATIVE THERAPY:
Surgery of paratenon of achilles tendon
Surgery by subachilles bursitis
Surgery by achilles tendon rupture
Reconstruction of the peroneal retinaculum or of the peroneal tendon
Reconstruction of the tibialis posterior retinaculum or tendon
