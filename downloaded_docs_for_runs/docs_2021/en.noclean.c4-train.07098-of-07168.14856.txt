You are recommended to take this medication exactly as it was indicated for you. Do not exceed the medication dosage. Do not take it for longer period of time than it was recommended. This medication is recommended to be taken orally irrespectively of food, as soon as the first migraine symptom appears. If you do not feel relief, do not double a dose, consult the doctor. If your feel a little better, or if you have headache again, you may take another drug dose at least in two hours, or as indicated by your doctor. Imitrex is recommended as 200 milligrams a day. Do not exceed this standard dose unless otherwise indicated by your doctor.
If you use the nasal spray of Imitrex, clear your nasal passages well. After using the spray, try not to blow your nose. If you use injection form, do not exceed a standard injection dose of six mg subcutaneously injected. The second dose may be required with at least one hour gap.
Imitrex is indicated only for treatment of an acute migraine attack or cluster headache. Do not take this drug for unusual types of migraine.
Drug Precautions
Before using this medication, Inform your doctor if you have ever had any kind of allergic reactions to the drug or main components, or to other triptan drugs, such as zolmitriptan, rizatriptan or if you have any other type of allergies. Before using Imitrex, consult your doctor if you have a history of cardiovascular disease, including heart attack, chest pain, stroke, transient ischemic attack, or blood circulation disease, high blood pressure, basilar migraine, or liver disorder.
Do not combine Imitrex with sibutramine or selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors, it may increase the serotonin level in the brain, leading to serotonin-related side effects. Imitrex is penetrated in breast milk, so avoid this drug during breast-feeding. Discuss the risks with your doctor regarding using this drug during pregnancy.
