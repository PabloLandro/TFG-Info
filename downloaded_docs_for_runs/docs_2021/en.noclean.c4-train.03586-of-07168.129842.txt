Canesten Once Daily Bifonazole Body Cream
Canesten Once Daily Bifonazole Body Cream is a convenient and effective treatment for fungal conditions of the body such as ringworm, jock itch, fungal skin rash and other tinea conditions. For treating athlete's foot, Canesten Bifonazole Once Daily Athlete's Foot cream is recommended.
This is the most convenient treatment option and only needs to be applied once a day. Use for two weeks after symptoms disappear to prevent recurrence.
Canesten Once Daily Bifonazole Body Cream contains Bifonazole 10 mg/g.
Available in pharmacies only.
