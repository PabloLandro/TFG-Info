Medial Ankle Sprain
Aka: Medial Ankle Sprain, Deltoid ligament rupture
See Also
Lateral Ankle Sprain
High Ankle Sprain
Epidemiology
Rare
Pathophysiology
Deltoid ligament injury
Deltoid injury requires major Trauma
Associated with Pott's Fracture-Subluxation
Exam
Medial deltoid ligament tenderness does not predict deep deltoid ligament disruption
Imaging
Gravity stress film
May be indicated by orthopedics to identify medial ankle dynamic instability
