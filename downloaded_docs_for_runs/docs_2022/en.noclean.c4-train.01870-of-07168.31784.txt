ABC NEWS - The Dangers of Vaccines Part 1 Autism Mercury MMR HPV Thimerosal
This news report exposes the fact that the preservative used in vaccines called Thimerosal, which contains mercury and has been linked to autism, is still being used in several vaccines including flu and tetanus shots, even though parents are being told that the mercury was removed. A congressional hearing concluded that the mercury in vaccines do cause autism. The U.S. government paid, as of this report, 1,322 parents for brain damage that vaccines caused their children. Unfortunately, the government refuses to report how many of those cases involved autism. This news report also notes that the Amish don't believe in vaccines and out of 22,000 Amish children only 4 had autism. Three of those had been vaccinated and the fourth was exposed to mercury from a coal plant. Several doctors in this report state their belief that vaccines cause autism, including Dr. Boyd Haley. The former Director of the National Institute of Health, Bernadine Healy, admits that a link between vaccines and autism should not be ruled out.

MSNBC - Robert F. Kennedy Jr. Explains Link Between Autism and Vaccines
In this interview Robert F. Kennedy Jr. discusses the medical data linking vaccines to the epidemic of autism. He exposes the fact that the increase in the number of vaccines children received in the late 1980's increased their exposure to mercury by 400% which is well above the EPA's guidelines for "safe" levels of mercury to be in the body. He also points out that there were no studies done on the effects on babies and children being exposed to so much additional mercury. It is important to keep in mind that mercury is the second most toxic substance on the planet after uranium. This means that the venom from a rattlesnake is less dangerous than the mercury they are giving to 1 day old babies. Kennedy also notes that when mercury was first added to vaccines in the 1930's that is when the first cases of autism started to appear.
