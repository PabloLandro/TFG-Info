Many Uses for WD40 Around the House
FIX IT WITH WD-40 !
What is WD-40? WD-40 stands for "Water Displacement, 40th attempt"
Bet you didn't know WD-40 would do this:
Protects silver from tarnishing
Keeps flies off cows
Removes lipstick stains
Removes dirt & grime from barbecue grill
Keeps glass shower doors free of water spots
Removes tomato stains from clothing
Removes "bug guts" from your car
Keeps bathroom mirror from fogging
Keeps pigeons off the balcony (they hate the smell)
Folks even spray it on their arms, hands & knees to relieve arthritis pain
Use it for fire ant bites. It takes the sting away
Of course, it lubricates almost anything
