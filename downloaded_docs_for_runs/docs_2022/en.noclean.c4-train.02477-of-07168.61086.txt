Self-Heating Magnetic Wrist Wrap – Set of Two
Thermal, self-heating magnetic wrist wrap with 4 neodymium magnets 2000 Gauss each and self-heating bio-composite that contains natural tourmaline mineral.
The wrist wrap when in contact with skin forms a magnetic field that resonates with human body and produces natural far infrared heat.
Effectively relieves wrist pain, improves blood circulation, helps to clear the meridians and enhance immunity.
How to use:
apply a small amount of water with a damp cloth or sponge to the middle of the neck support (the side that comes into contact with the skin)
Make sure that the wrap is in direct contact with your skin.
The heat starts generating after about 15-20 minutes
Please note that the heat might be very intense for the first 15 minutes. It might be a bit uncomfortable but it is very safe and won’t burn your skin. The effect is similar to the deep heat ointment rub. If the heat gets too uncomfortable, you can slightly relax the wrap around the wrist.
After about 15 minutes, the heat intensity lowers to a more pleasant warm sensation.
Magnetic wrist wrap can be left on for a few hours or even for the night.
Wrist wraps can be hand washed in soapy lukewarm water.
